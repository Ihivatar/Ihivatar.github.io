Boxsweeper Information

This was done relatively early on in my Computer Science education as a way
to increase the difficulty over the original (more tiles / bombs) and learn
about the various methods of generating and clearing a randomized board.

Left Ctrl + Left Mouse Click and drag drags the map around
Right click cycles flags on tile
Left click reveals tile
Mouse wheel scroll zoom in/out
