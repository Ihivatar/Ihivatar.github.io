Galactic Repairs Gallery

IMAGES COMING SOON!


Near the end of February 2016, I joined an international Indie Game Development team
whose goal was to create a small game that everyone could use for their portfolio
and for everyone to get used to working together. After it is complete, the current plan
is to work on a full length game.

Due to the early stages of development, I unfortunately don't have much in the way of
images to share yet, as a lot of the art assets are still being worked on.

This game is being made in Unreal Engine 4.10.4 by a team comprised of members from
around the world and we are using Perforce as our source control system.

Game setting:

You are an engineer that is sent to planets to repair beacons owned by the
company you work for, Galactic Repairs. Most of these planets are friendly,
but occasionally you have to defend yourself from indigenous life. Today you
find yourself on such a planet.

My Contributions

Here are some of the features I've implemented into the game:
  • Decoy system where the player can temporarily spawn an "illusion" to destract enemies.
  • Placeable turret that doesn't allow placement while colldiing with other objects and turns red to inform player of that.
  • Trigger volume that spawns a "drop pod" and shakes the screen until it crashes into the ground, at which point it is immobile.
  • Trigger volume that interpolates the camera pitch to a target pitch until the player leaves the volume.
  • Portal functionality, (teleportation).
  • Various bug fixes.
